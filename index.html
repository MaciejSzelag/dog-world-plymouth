About Me

Hello!
My name is
Sylwia.

I am a certified dog trainer with many years of experience. My passion for working with dogs began many years ago when I adopted my first dog, Misiek. Thatâ€™s when I realized how important proper communication and understanding between a dog and its owner are. At six months old, Misiek had a problem with resource guarding. He would bite anyone who tried to touch his things, such as his bowl or toys. Misiek was taken away from his previous owner.

In my work, I focus on positive training methods that build trust and a bond between humans and dogs. I believe that every dog, regardless of breed or age, can learn new things and improve its behaviour with patience, consistency, and love. I offer individual training sessions, group classes, and behavioural consultations. My goal is to help solve behavioural problems, teach basic commands, and support owners in the everyday challenges of caring for their dogs.

Feel free to contact me, and let's work together to make your dog a well-behaved and happy member of your family!
